Medical AI Bot

Designing trust, clarity, and calm in a medical AI assistant.

The Problem

Medical information is overwhelming and filled with jargon. Users seeking guidance are often anxious, and poor UX increases confusion.

Understanding the Users

Non-technical users looking for quick, understandable medical guidance. Their biggest needs were clarity, trust, and reassurance.

The UX Challenge

How can an AI system provide insights without overwhelming or frightening users?

Design Decisions

A conversational flow, minimal UI, and step-by-step information to reduce cognitive load and anxiety.

The Solution

A Medical AI Bot that guides users through symptoms, provides condition insights, drug recommendations, and interaction checks in simple language.

Reflection

This project taught me that in healthcare, good UX is not optional — it is ethical.

How I Think

  • Clarity over complexity
  • Empathy over assumptions
  • Function before decoration
  • Design for emotional context
