Case Study — Medical AI Bot

Designing trust, clarity, and responsibility in a medical AI assistant.

The Problem

Medical information is overwhelming and often filled with jargon. Users seeking guidance are usually anxious, and poorly designed interfaces can increase fear instead of reducing it.

Understanding the Users

The primary users were non-technical individuals looking for quick, understandable medical guidance. Their main concerns were trust, clarity, and knowing what to do next.

The UX Challenge

How can an AI system provide medical insights without overwhelming users or making them feel unsafe?

Design Decisions

I focused on a simple conversational flow, step-by-step information delivery, and calm visual hierarchy to reduce cognitive load.

The Solution

The Medical AI Bot guides users through symptoms, provides possible condition insights, recommends drugs, and checks interactions — all using clear, human-friendly language.

Reflection

This project reinforced that in healthcare, good UX is not optional. Clear communication and empathy are as important as accuracy.
