Case Study — Medical AI Bot

Designing trust and responsibility in a medical AI assistant.

The Problem

Medical information is overwhelming and filled with jargon. Users seeking guidance are already anxious.

Users

Non-technical users who need quick, understandable medical guidance and clear next steps.

The UX Challenge

How can AI provide medical insights without increasing fear or confusion?

Design Decisions

Conversational flow, step-by-step information, and a calm visual hierarchy.

The Solution

A Medical AI Bot that guides users through symptoms, provides insights, recommends drugs, and checks interactions using simple language.

Reflection

In healthcare, good UX is ethical. Clarity and empathy matter as much as accuracy.

How I Think

Clarity over complexity Empathy before assumptions Design for emotional context Technology should explain itself
